What I build

annoyance → built

Spotify queue bot

A shared Spotify link instantly starts playing and destroys the whole queue. Every single time.

Built: forward the song via WhatsApp → the bot appends it cleanly to the queue → it plays when it is supposed to. No interruptions. A lot of my projects start exactly like this: I get annoyed, then I automate the problem away.

legal tech

AI for energy law

German energy law is complex, fragmented, and difficult to search through.

Built: an AI tool that makes this complexity more navigable instead of leaving it locked behind specialist knowledge.

research

Predicting pyrolysis yields

Lab experiments are expensive, and it is hard to understand which conditions lead to which yields.

Built: machine learning models (including Random Forest and XGBoost) that predict yields from literature data — carefully validated against leakage.

freelance

AI tools for clients

A lot of teams know AI could help them — they just do not know where to start.

Built: DSGVO-compliant RAG customer support for KooKoo GmbH, browser automations for a medical practice, and n8n/MCP-based workflows that remove repetitive admin work.
